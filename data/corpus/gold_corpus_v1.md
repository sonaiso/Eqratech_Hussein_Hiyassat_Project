# Gold Corpus V1 - Arabic Morphological Analysis

## Sentence 1
**Text**: `الْكِتَابُ عَلَى الطَّاوِلَةِ`
**Translation**: The book is on the table

### Word 1: الْكِتَابُ
- **Root**: ك-ت-ب (k-t-b)
- **Pattern**: فِعَال (VERBAL_NOUN)
- **Prefix**: ال (definite article)
- **Case**: Nominative (ُ)
- **POS**: Noun

### Word 2: عَلَى
- **Root**: ع-ل-و (approximate)
- **POS**: Preposition
- **Category**: PREP

### Word 3: الطَّاوِلَةِ
- **Root**: ط-و-ل
- **Pattern**: فَاعِلَة
- **Prefix**: ال
- **Case**: Genitive (ِ)
- **POS**: Noun
